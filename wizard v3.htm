Formal Notification Script

Your appointment has been booked for Tuesday, 13 December 2016 at 9:00 AM.

This appointment will take place at MAXEmployment Stage 2 CASCOM Centre 13-17 Scaturchio Street,CASUARINA.

The purpose of this appointment is to discuss your ongoing job search requirements and activities or services that will help you to find work. You may also be required to review and agree to updates to your Job Plan.

This is a notice under social security law. This means that you must attend the appointment on Tuesday, 13 December 2016 at 9:00 AM to keep getting your income support payment.  If you don't attend, and don't accept a Job Plan if required, your payment may be stopped or you may lose money.

If you can't attend for any reason, you need to contact us before the appointment start time on 08 8999  0300 to let us know the reason why you can't attend. If you don't call us before the appointment, your income support payment may still be stopped or you may lose money even if you think you have a good reason for not being able to attend.

Can you confirm that you understand the requirement to attend this appointment and the possible consequences of non-attendance?

Appointment with MAX Employment CASUARINA on Tuesday, 13 December 2016

You must attend the appointment outlined below in return for Parenting Payment Single. You are required to attend an appointment with us. The purpose of this appointment is to discuss your ongoing job search requirements and activities or services that will help you to find work. At this appointment you may also be required to review and agree to updates to your Job Plan.

1. What do I have to do?

Time: 9:00 AM

2. What if I am unable to attend the appointment at this time?

You need to call max employment casuarina on 08 8999  0300 before the appointment with a good reason so that another time can be made.

If you do not advise beforehand if you cannot attend, your payment may be stopped even if you have a good reason for missing the appointment. You may also lose some payment. Not complying with requirements like this can result in you losing your income support payment for up to eight weeks if you are deliberately avoiding your mutual obligation requirements.

3. What will happen at the appointment?

  • We will discuss the services and assistance that we can provide you with, including those that are already part of your Job Plan and any changes to these services
  • We may discuss your job search and/or participation in activities as required in your Job Plan since your last appointment
  • You may be provided with assistance about the best ways to look for and apply for a job, including how to access Self-help facilities
  • You may be referred to appropriate job vacancies
  • We may identify any relevant work experience, training or other vocational or non-vocational interventions you may need since your last appointment, and you will be referred to these as required

4. What should I consider bringing to the appointment?

  • Your job search efforts
  • Details of any upcoming or recent job interviews
  • Any feedback you have received from employers in regards to job applications
  • Any new employment-related documents

5. Questions?

You need to call max employment casuarina on 08 8999  0300 before the appointment if:

  • you cannot attend the appointment at the time specified;
  • you have questions about this letter; or
  • you have special requirements for the appointment (such as an interpreter or special building access).

Please treat all communication from us very seriously, because it is the same as being notified of your requirements by Centrelink.
